Our Story

Empowering Small Businesses for Global Growth

IntegriBiz was born out of a desire to enlighten people about the often-overlooked aspects of business growth. We are a small, dedicated team based in Cape Town, South Africa, passionate about providing a solid starting point for businesses aiming to scale.

Our project began with the idea of helping small local businesses navigate the complexities of ISO compliance. We realized that adhering to ISO standards not only simplifies meeting local regulatory requirements but also sets a foundation for global commercial success.

  • Why ISO Standards?

    ISO standards are globally recognized, providing a framework that helps businesses meet local and international regulations. By complying with ISO standards, you streamline your vendor processes and open up opportunities for growth and diversification.

  • Our Inspiration

    Inspired by the need to address "the stuff that no one talks about," we created IntegriBiz to guide businesses through ISO compliance, ensuring their documentation and processes are always up-to-date and ready for global expansion.
